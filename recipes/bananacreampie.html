Banana Cream Pie

Bananas, but in a pie

This recipe is about a pie. A pie that was once a humble blueberry but had a dream of becoming much more.

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Salt
  • Egg Yolks
  • Pie Shell
  • Butter
  • Vanilla
  • Bananas

Steps

  1. Sprinkle the berries with sugar, cornstarch, cinnamon, and salt.
  2. Pour the filling into the botton pie crust and finish with a lattice crust on top.
  3. Bake until the filling is bubbling and the crust is golden.
